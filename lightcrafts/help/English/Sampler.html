Sampler

Sharing space with the ZoneFinder, Color Selection Viewer, and Histogram, in the upper-right corner of the LightZone window is also the Sampler that shows information about the pixel in your photo that is directly under the mouse. As you move the mouse, the information updates.

To show the Sampler:

  1. Click the Sampler tab.
