ワークフローの統合

While LightZone is an all-in-one application for your digital photography workflow from raw conversion to printing, you can integrate LightZone into your existing workflow with other applications if you prefer.

Notably, LightZone saves the tool stack as part of your edited photos' metadata. This allows you to edit photos across multiple sessions each time picking up from where you left off.

LightZone と以下のアプリケーションを一緒に使う方法について知りたければ、関連するリンクをクリックします。

  • Aperture (Apple 製)
  • iPhoto (Apple 製)
  • iView MediaPro (マイクロソフト製)
  • Lightroom (アドビ システムズ製)
