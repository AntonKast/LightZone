ZoneMapper: Finding zones

2 of 8

The ZoneMapper tool works with the ZoneFinder: as you move the mouse over the zones in a ZoneMapper, the corresponding zones in the ZoneFinder highlight in yellow.

Using a ZoneMapper tool with the ZoneFinder allows you to find zones in your photo quickly in order to make adjustments.

For example, you can quickly find both the white and black points in your photo allowing you to fix the exposure and get deeper blacks.

for at finde zones i dit billede

  1. Klik på ZoneMapper værktøjs knappen i værktøjs linien. (Et nyt ZoneMapper vil fremkomme i toppen af Værktøjs stakken.
  2. Move the mouse over the zones in the ZoneMapper. (As you do so, the corresponding zones in the ZoneFinder will highlight.)
